Fortune LeadX: A CRM built for
real estate.

A Sales CRM designed from scratch for real estate sales teams. Sole designer responsible for the entire product design lifecycle — from discovery to pixel-perfect handoff.

Role

Sole UI/UX Designer

Timeline

Sep 2025 – Present

Platform

Web Application

Tools

Design System

Built from scratch

Act I · The Conflict

The Problem

Real estate sales teams were drowning in generic CRMs — tools built for tech startups, not property deals. Agents needed pipeline visibility and workflow automation tailored to how property sales actually work.

Act II · Behind the Scenes

The Process

Started with stakeholder interviews and sales team shadowing to map real workflows. Built user flows from scratch, ran multiple wireframe rounds, and iterated to high-fidelity prototypes.

Act III · The Final Cut

The Solution

A clean, modular CRM with context-aware dashboards, drag-and-drop pipeline views, and a design system that extended to HRM modules. A tool teams actually wanted to open in the morning.
